Creator

Hello! I'm Te, I am the creator and only person behind TheFunkySullsNFT project.

To tell you a little bit about me, I moved to NOLA about 7 years ago, i work in construction and refurbish computers as my side gig, I drop-out of school and started working when I was 13 (so please excuse my grammar) and since then I have worked doing many different things from filleting fish to machine operator to waiting tables. I bought my first computer back in 2002 and that's when my passion for learning started, i first learned how to hack the fat-PSP and let me tell you that was a lot of fun, adding custom themes and being able to play Nintendo Roms in a Sony device that was amazing, if anyone reading this remember the process... you'd have to make a “Pandora's Battery by shorting it and then create a “magic memory stick”. not as easy as it sounds! what a times... Anyway, that's a story for another time.

Few years later i bought an iPhone and of course i have to jailbreak it and so i did around that time was when then I started learning to repair computers and small electronincs and it's been my side gig ever since. I learned about Cryptocurrency and Bitcoin back then and I remember even getting a wallet but who knows why but i din't even get one Satoshi.. Oh well!.

I had an interest in creating apps for iPhones but couldn't afford an apple computer and that's when i built my first Hackintosh. but between work a girlfriend and life in general, i didn't really have much time to spare so I put it to the side. since then we broken up and I moved to NOLA and i've been working in construction for the past 7 years but always learning little bits about tech crypto and NFTs.

One thing that I inherited from my Dad before he died when I was a kid was his love for Rock&Roll and Skulls, so I have always loved to draw skulls(while listening to rock.. really!)

Over a year ago i got Covid so i had to lock myself in my room and that's when i started creating TheFunkySkullsNFT project.

I'd tried finding a partner but in my line of work I can only say it would've been easier finding an investor for an easy-to-pull-wedgie-pants™ (Hello, Kanye!) than finding anyone interested in join me, but hey! here I am and the project is finish and I'm very proud and in fact I'm already playing with Blender for version 2.0 "3D Full-Body Animated"

So I paved my own road and learned everything i could from setting up images and create a collection to the Solidy language to write the Smart-Contract to HTML-CSS-JS to more or less make a usable website and yeap! its been a upwards road but I can say that I am very proud and Grateful for how Wonderful the NFT family has been to me and how of an Amazing experience everything has turned out to be.

Thank you all!.
